About Boss Engineering Nigeria Limited

We are a civil engineering company specialising in rooftop solar installations, transformer siting/planting, solar streetlights, GSM repair training and installation services across Nigeria.

With more than 15+ years of hands-on experience working across states, our team has executed top-roof solar installations for residential and commercial clients, safely planted and commissioned distribution transformers, and delivered training programs in solar installation and GSM repairs to technical teams and community technicians.

  • > Rooftop solar system design & installation
  • > Transformer siting and safe planting
  • > Solar streetlight networks (design & deployment)
  • > GSM repair & field technician training
  • > Project management and safety compliance

We focus on durable, maintainable installations and on-sharing practical skills with local technicians so projects are sustainable and locally supported.
